## 🚀 About This Project

**Class-12-ISC** is a structured academic repository designed to
organize ISC Class 12 study resources subject-wise.\
It acts as a centralized digital library for efficient preparation and
long-term academic organization.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🧠 Subjects Covered

### 📊 Economics

-   Micro & Macro Economics Notes\
-   Important 2/3/5/10 Mark Questions\
-   Case Study Practice\
-   Graph-Based Questions

### 📈 Accounts

-   Journal Entries\
-   Ledger Posting\
-   Trial Balance\
-   Financial Statements\
-   Partnership & Company Accounts

### 📚 Business Studies

-   Chapter-wise Summaries\
-   Most Asked Questions\
-   Case Studies\
-   Important Differences & Definitions

### 🏢 Commerce

-   Concept Notes\
-   Frequently Asked Questions\
-   Short Answer Preparation
